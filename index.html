Our Heritage

Traditional Craftsmanship

AARI WORKS preserves the centuries-old tradition of Aari embroidery, an exquisite art form that originated in the royal courts of India. Our skilled artisans use a specialized hook-like needle to create intricate designs with precision and care.

Each piece is meticulously handcrafted, taking days and sometimes weeks to complete, ensuring a unique treasure that tells a story of cultural heritage and artistic excellence.
